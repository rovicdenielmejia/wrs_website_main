Our Recruitment Process

Transparent, efficient, and results-driven—from brief to placement.

Recruitment process steps

1

Brief & Requirements

We gather your hiring needs—role, skills, experience, timeline, and culture fit. A dedicated recruiter reviews and confirms the scope.

2

Sourcing & Screening

We source candidates from our talent pool, job boards, and networks. Initial screening via resume review and phone/video calls.

3

Shortlisting & Presentation

Shortlisted profiles are presented with CVs and summary notes. You review and select candidates for interview.

4

Interviews & Assessment

We coordinate interview schedules, support video interviews, and conduct skill or behavioral assessments as needed.

5

Offer & Onboarding

We assist with offer negotiation, background checks, and onboarding coordination. Full support until your hire is settled.
