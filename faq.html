Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our recruitment and HR services.

For Employers

How long does recruitment take? We aim for a faster turnaround; many clients see around 25% improvement compared to DIY hiring. Timeline depends on role and volume.

What industries do you serve? We serve BPO, corporate, healthcare, tech, retail, education, finance, and more, locally and internationally.

How do employers get charged? We offer transparent packages (e.g. per-hire or custom partnership). See our Solutions page for pricing options, or request a proposal.

How do I post a job? Contact us for a consultation. We can post roles for you and support your hiring end to end.

For Job Seekers

Is there a placement fee for job seekers? No. Our recruitment services are free for job seekers.

How do I apply? Browse Jobs, choose a role, and use the Apply Now button to submit your application and resume. You can also book a consultation for resume and career support.

Do you offer overseas jobs? Yes. We list local and international roles, including remote and relocation opportunities.
