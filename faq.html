Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our recruitment and HR services.

For Employers

How do I post a job? Contact us for a consultation. We can post roles for you and support your hiring.

What areas do you recruit for? We support remote and nationwide recruitment in the Philippines, plus international placements (e.g. KSA, UAE).

What is your typical time-to-hire? We aim for a faster turnaround; many clients see around 25% improvement compared to DIY hiring.

For Job Seekers

How do I apply? Browse Jobs, choose a role, and submit your application with your resume. You can also book a consultation for resume and career support.

Do you charge candidates? No. Our recruitment services are free for job seekers.

Do you offer overseas jobs? Yes. We list local and international roles, including remote and relocation opportunities.
